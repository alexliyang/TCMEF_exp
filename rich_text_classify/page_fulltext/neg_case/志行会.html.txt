Since its establishment in March 2009, BTP has already enrolled over 30 executive members, over 80 alumnis, and over 900 volunteers involved, and has launched five independent projects including in Ghana Africa, Hainan, Hong Kong, Sichuan and Yunnan.BTP devotes to establishing a platform for volunteers and social workers, in order to cultivate participants’ social responsibility and global citizenship.Furthermore, BTP endeavored to improve the living standard as well as education level, health care, and culture preservation of the target areas. Additionally, BTP collects all kinds of information through the researches within its projects. That information will be converted into analytical reports, and to be distributed during public exhibitions, in order to promote the concept of volunteer service.志行会宗旨编辑志乎万物，行济天下（凭借宽广包容的志愿者精神，以实际行动改变世界）Think Beyond, Act BeyondTo bring changes in the world by acting with the spirit of volunteers.志行会历史编辑BTP成立于2009年，并下属香港大学“学生机构孵化计划”，成立至今已筹划开展了六个独立的志愿者项目（非洲加纳，中国海南、四川、香港、云南，内蒙古）。BTP 现有干事会成员33 人，累计干事会成员达200 余人，累计约500名志愿者参与活动。志行会目标编辑创造更多的志愿者活动机会To create more opportunities for existing and potential volunteers.改善受助地区的生活水平To improve the living standard of target areas.促进各地区文化交流To encourage the inter-cultural exchanges among diverse regions.普及志愿服务理念To promote the concept of volunteer service.志行会项目成果编辑BTP 已开展的六个长期志愿项目均取得了卓越的成绩，例如非洲团队获得加纳副总统接见; 海南及四川团队获得当地媒体的头条报道; BTP 也曾入选香港大学“学生机构孵育计划”等。并且，各项目均获得香港大学Service 100 基金支持。在六个志愿者项目中，教授文化课和素质拓展类课程教学是常规活动。除此之外，各项目组继承传统并再创新，开展了诸多特色活动以满足该项目组受助人群的需求。另外，除了每年暑假定期的支教项目，BTP还与非政府组织合作举办体验活动，例如Struggle for Survival，让参与者体验高度真实的“极度贫穷的一天”，以增加对弱势群体的关注与同理心，另外更有Dialogue in the Dark 体验旅程。参考资料1.关于我们|Beyond the Pivot．Beyond the Pivot[引用日期2016-07-08]词条标签：文化
